MadelineProto
MadelineProto is a client/server library for the Telegram messenger, with full support for every user-API method, truly LOADS of abstractions, and async logic in the alpha version.
It also supports playing and recording VoIP telegram phone calls, secret chats, and much more!
Head over to tutorial.madelineproto.xyz for a full installation and usage tutorial.
